What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is an invention marathon. BrickHack is a 24-hour in-person hackathon that is completely free and open to any university student.

Apply to come spend February 22nd and 23rd, 2025 with us to learn, collaborate, build, and innovate! Expect food, swag, mentorship, and timeless brick-laden memories!

Applying

Register an account via our application system to apply to BrickHack today! Applications will close by Wednesday, February 19th, 2025.

If your application is accepted, you will receive an email from our team with more information.

You must be 18+ and a current university student to apply. Applications and event attendance are completely free.

Participating

At BrickHack, students compete individually or in teams of 2-4 to build anything they want. You could make an app, a website, a game, a design, or any other form of interactive media!
The possibilities are endless.

No experience is required; we provide mentors to help you along the way!
